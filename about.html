Empowering City Creators

CityFame (cityfame.in) is a free, city-wise Instagram influencer directory designed to connect local creators directly with regional brands and businesses.

Our Mission

We believe hyper-local micro-influencers carry genuine trust within their communities. CityFame bridges the gap between Solapur creators and local business owners—without high commission fees or middlemen agencies.

Expanding Across Cities

Starting strong in Solapur, Maharashtra, CityFame is preparing to launch in Pune, Mumbai, Kolhapur, Nagpur, and major tier-2 & tier-3 cities across India.
